 ‘’Ezypto Token’’

EZP Token

Ezypto token(EZP)’s mission is to provide a new digital payment system for online and in-person shopping. Shoppers can earn rewards using this digital asset for their online transactions under Ezypto gateway and with no commissions.

Learn more

Goals and rewards

Ezypto token (EZP) serves several purposes, most important of which to provide customers with an unlimited, unique payment method. It can be used for shopping various goods, daily necessities, even your dream home or car. You can use the token to pay in online stores / Ezypto POS.

Learn more

Ezypto POS

Also shown in the roadmap, our Ezypto smart POS devices are the best of their kind to provide fast, safe and reliable transactions through online and in-store shopping. 7Payments can be made via biometric and fingerprint or through the mobile app using the Ezypto Token(EZP) in a fraction of a second. Plus, customers will earn rewards for each and every transaction they make through the Ezypto system.

Learn more

The Future of Ezypto Token

We believe a new revolution is under way in the digital payment system, with the development efforts and enterprises now taking place in the Ezypto world. And as more and more transactions are made using the Ezypto token, the value of Ezypto assets will balloon in no time. A new era in digital payments is now much closer to all of us than any other time.

How to purchase Ezypto Token

Right now, you can buy and trade Ezypto token (EZP) either in Pancakeswap exchange (BEP20)(https://pancakeswap.finance/swap) or in Uniswap exchange (ERC20) (https://app.uniswap.org/#/swap). Please follow us on social media for any news and updates on the listing of Ezypto token in new exchanges.

Be careful of all the fake and scam tokens out there using the registered names if the legitimate tokens. Make sure to search for the real Ezypto token (EZP) and use only the 2 provided addresses to add Ezypto token (EZP) into your digital wallet.
